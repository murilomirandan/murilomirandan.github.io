Red-footed tortoise

Over-collection has caused them to be vulnerable to extinction.

  • Scientific name: Chelonoidis carbonaria
  • Average size: 30 cm (12 in)
  • Diet: omnivorous, mostly fuit
  • Distribution: northern South America

On average, tortoises are generally 30 cm as adults, but can reach over 40 cm (16 in). They have dark-colored, loaf-shaped carapaces with a lighter patch in the middle of each scute, and dark limbs with brightly colored scales that range from pale yellow to dark red.
